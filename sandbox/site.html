Cayos Tours – Site Plan

Site Name

Cayos Tours

This name represents a tourism company offering guided tours, snorkeling adventures, and island experiences in Cayos Cochinos, Honduras.

Optional domain: cayostours.com (example)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this project is to promote tourism to Cayos Cochinos by providing visitors with accessible, informative, and visually appealing content. The website will help users plan their trip, discover activities, and understand how to respect and preserve the natural environment.

Scenarios

  • How much does a full-day tour to Cayos Cochinos cost?
  • What activities are included in the snorkeling package?
  • How can I book a tour date online?

Color Scheme

Primary Color – #0077b6: Used for headings, buttons, and the navigation bar.

Accent Color – #ffdd57: Used for highlights, icons, and accent details.

Typography

Font 1: Poppins – Used for headings.

Font 2: Open Sans – Used for body text and paragraphs.
